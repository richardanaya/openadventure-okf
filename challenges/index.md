# Challenges

* [Appendix C: Challenge Library](overview.md) - The Challenge Library includes a collection of pre-built Challenges which demonstrate ways to use the Core Rules to create an exciting Encounter.
* [Build a Shelter](build-a-shelter.md) - A storm is coming and the characters must build a shelter for protection.
* [Calm the Mob](calm-the-mob.md) - The characters must stop an angry mob from executing an innocent man due to a misunderstanding.
* [Clear the Cave-in](clear-the-cave-in.md) - The characters must clear the cave-in that blocks their path.
* [Disease: Filth Fever](disease-filth-fever.md) - The character contracts filth fever.
* [Gather Information](gather-information.md) - The party scours the city to gather information about an evil cult that is sacrificing kidnapped citizens.
* [Guard Station](guard-station.md) - A pair of guards prevent the characters from gaining entrance to the castle.
* [Navigate the Forest](navigate-the-forest.md) - The characters must find their way through a dense forest.
* [Poison: Fireblood](poison-fireblood.md) - A brutal injected poison that makes the victim feel like their veins are burning.
* [Poison: Stonefoot](poison-stonefoot.md) - An injected poison that makes it difficult to walk or run.
* [Research](research.md) - The Royal University gave the characters access to their library to find information on the Dark Lord’s origins.
* [Sinking Ship](sinking-ship.md) - The small boat in which the characters are traveling struck a reef. Now it is taking on water.
* [Survive the Avalanche](survive-the-avalanche.md) - An avalanche is bearing down on the heroes and they must scramble to avoid an icy doom.
* [Towering Cliff](towering-cliff.md) - The characters must reach the top of a towering cliff.
* [Trap: Deadfall](trap-deadfall.md) - A pile of rocks tumble from the ceiling and land on the victim.
* [Trap: Pit](trap-pit.md) - A 10-foot-deep pit opens beneath the victim.
* [Trap: Spiked Pit](trap-spiked-pit.md) - A 10-foot-deep pit with spikes lining the bottom opens beneath the victim.
* [Wind Tunnel](wind-tunnel.md) - Clever engineering fills this tunnel with a powerful wind that holds the characters back.
