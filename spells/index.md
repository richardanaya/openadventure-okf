# Spells

* [Spell Library](overview.md) - Catalogs 15 pre-built spells and explains each entry's casting details, game effect, and design rationale.
* [Animate Vines](animate-vines.md) - Animates nearby vines to Grab a target and, while maintained, perform other Actions allowed against a Grabbed target.
* [Arcane Bolt](arcane-bolt.md) - Fires a bolt of blue energy that reduces the target's Body Points or Challenge Points, subject to Armor (Body).
* [Arcane Shield](arcane-shield.md) - Creates a maintained magical shield that increases the Spellcaster's Body Defense against blockable attacks.
* [Bull’s Strength](bulls-strength.md) - Surrounds a target with a bull-like nimbus that increases Strength while the spell is maintained.
* [Ethereal Hand](ethereal-hand.md) - Creates a maintained ethereal hand that manipulates nearby objects and performs simple one-handed tasks.
* [Fear](fear.md) - Attacks the Spirit of creatures in an area, forcing those reduced to zero points to flee while the spell is maintained.
* [Fireball](fireball.md) - Explodes at a target space, dealing fire damage to creatures in the area that fail a Reaction Check.
* [Grease](grease.md) - Coats an area in maintained magical grease, requiring a Major Action to move through it.
* [Heal Wounds](heal-wounds.md) - Heals one wound of a selected type using a Spellcasting Check with the same base difficulty and casting time as mundane treatment.
* [Light](light.md) - Makes a held object glow as brightly as a lit torch after a successful Spellcasting Check.
* [Recover Body Points](recover-body-points.md) - On a successful Spellcasting Action Check, the Target recovers a number of Body Points equal to the Effect of the spell, up to the target’s maximum points.
* [Sleep](sleep.md) - Attacks the Mind of creatures in an area, putting those reduced to zero points into a deep but non-wounding sleep.
* [Teleport](teleport.md) - Instantly moves the Spellcaster to a visible location within a Stone Throw without crossing the intervening space.
* [Turn Undead](turn-undead.md) - Forces targeted undead to remain at least Across the Room from the Spellcaster while the spell is maintained.
* [Wall Crawler](wall-crawler.md) - Uses a Spellcasting Check to climb a wall or cliff and reduce any associated Challenge Points.
