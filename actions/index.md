# Actions

* [Action Library](overview.md) - Catalogs 51 example Actions and explains how to adapt their rules to the circumstances and fiction of play.
* [Aid](aid.md) - Create a positive condition that adds a Benefit to an Action Score.
* [Assist](assist.md) - Raise an Action Score by the Effect by contributing directly to the Action.
* [Attack: Area of Effect](attack-area-of-effect.md) - Make an attack that strikes every person in a particular area.
* [Attack: Melee or Ranged](attack-melee-or-ranged.md) - Attack a foe with a readied weapon to reduce their Challenge Points.
* [Attack: Special](attack-special.md) - Attack a foe in a creative way to reduce their Challenge Points.
* [Balance](balance.md) - Walk along a narrow surface or stand on a small platform without falling.
* [Break Through](break-through.md) - Smash through a barrier such as a door or wall.
* [Climb](climb.md) - Climb up or over a barrier.
* [Complicate](complicate.md) - Increase the Difficulty Score for one Action the target is attempting.
* [Coup de Grâce](coup-de-grace.md) - Finish off an Incapacitated, unconscious, or Defeated target.
* [Deceive](deceive.md) - Make the target believe something that is untrue.
* [Defend](defend.md) - Put all your efforts into defense to make it harder for enemies to hit you or an ally.
* [Detect Discrepancy](detect-discrepancy.md) - The character analyzes the target to identify physical elements that are out of place or incorrect.
* [Detect Motive](detect-motive.md) - Determine a character or creature’s true intentions.
* [Disarm](disarm.md) - Use combat skill to force an opponent to drop a wielded weapon or item.
* [Disarm Trap](disarm-trap.md) - Safely disarm a trap so it doesn’t harm you or your allies.
* [Disguise](disguise.md) - Fool people into believing you are something other than what you are by changing your appearance.
* [Drag](drag.md) - Use Strength to move an unwilling, Grabbed opponent.
* [Escape](escape.md) - Free yourself when Grabbed or Restrained.
* [Grab](grab.md) - Grab an enemy or an object the enemy is holding.
* [Harm](harm.md) - Injure a Grabbed opponent.
* [Harry](harry.md) - Distract the opponent allowing allies to add a Benefit to their Attack Action Scores.
* [Hide](hide.md) - Hide a person or object to avoid detection.
* [Impede](impede.md) - Impede a foe’s ability to move so they must use a Major Action to change positions.
* [Impersonate](impersonate.md) - Pretend to be a specific person or type of person when interacting with others.
* [Improve](improve.md) - Improve the Effect of a previous Action Check.
* [Intercept](intercept.md) - Throw yourself in front of an attack so it hits you instead of your ally.
* [Jump](jump.md) - Jumps over or across an obstacle or gap.
* [Lift](lift.md) - Lift a heavy object off the ground.
* [Manipulate](manipulate.md) - Make the target do something you desire.
* [Obstruct](obstruct.md) - Create a condition that adds a Drawback to a Difficulty Score.
* [Pick Lock](pick-lock.md) - Unlock a lock without the key.
* [Push/Pull](push-pull.md) - Move an especially heavy object.
* [Recovery](recovery.md) - Provide physical, mental, or emotional aid to restore Body, Mind, or Spirit Points.
* [Reduce Escalation](reduce-escalation.md) - Reduce the number of Challenge Points an Escalating Challenge increases each Round.
* [Remove Drawback](remove-drawback.md) - Remove or offset a condition that applies a Drawback to a Difficulty Score.
* [Restrain](restrain.md) - Pin a Grabbed opponent so the only Action they may attempt is Escape.
* [Ride](ride.md) - Control a horse or other mount under difficult circumstances.
* [Sacrifice](sacrifice.md) - Prevent an ally from being struck by acting as a human shield.
* [Search](search.md) - Actively search for something that is concealed, hidden, or difficult to notice.
* [Shove](shove.md) - Push an enemy from Reach to Across the Room.
* [Sneak](sneak.md) - Move around without being detected.
* [Swim](swim.md) - Keep from drowning when faced with dangerous circumstances in the water.
* [Swing](swing.md) - Swing on a rope, vine, or other hanging object.
* [Take](take.md) - Tear something you Grabbed away from an opponent.
* [Takedown](takedown.md) - Slam a Grabbed opponent into the ground to cause injury and leave them prone.
* [Throw Opponent](throw-opponent.md) - Toss a Grabbed opponent to a point Across the Room.
* [Track](track.md) - Follow a set of tracks.
* [Treat Wounds](treat-wounds.md) - Heal one of a character’s Wounds.
* [Trip](trip.md) - Trip a moving opponent so they fall prone.
* [Withdraw](withdraw.md) - Withdraw your character from an Encounter.
