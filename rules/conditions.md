# Conditions and Common States

This page collects frequently referenced states and defensive options. The linked rules remain authoritative.

## Incapacitated

A Player Character becomes **Incapacitated** when their Body, Mind, or Spirit Points reach 0. They are prone and disoriented, cannot normally make Action Checks other than Catch Your Breath Recovery Checks, and have a base Defense of 0. See [Injury, Wounds, and Healing](injury-wounds-and-healing.md#incapacitated).

## Dying

A character who fails a Death Check is unconscious and **Dying**. Unless stabilized, they die after a number of Rounds determined by the Primary Attribute associated with the Wound that triggered the Death Check. See [Death Checks and Dying](injury-wounds-and-healing.md#death-checks-and-dying).

## Defeated

An NPC or Adversary is **Defeated** when its Challenge Points reach 0. Defeat may mean death, surrender, flight, humiliation, or another outcome appropriate to the Actions used against it. See [Adversaries](challenges/adversaries.md#defeated-adversaries).

## Prone

A **prone** character is lying on the ground. Individual Actions specify relevant effects; for example, a prone target grants a Benefit to a [Restrain](../actions/restrain.md) Check. The Adventure Guide adjudicates other effects using the [Rule of Common Sense](guidance-for-guides/rule-of-common-sense.md).

## Grabbed

A **Grabbed** character may still act, but must [Escape](../actions/escape.md) before moving normally. The grabber may maintain the Grab or follow it with Actions such as [Drag](../actions/drag.md), [Harm](../actions/harm.md), [Restrain](../actions/restrain.md), or [Throw Opponent](../actions/throw-opponent.md). See [Grab](../actions/grab.md).

## Restrained

A **Restrained** character cannot perform Major or Minor Actions requiring movement except [Escape](../actions/escape.md). A successful Escape reduces Restrained to Grabbed. See [Restrain](../actions/restrain.md).

## Slowed or Impeded

A character affected by a slowing or impeding condition must use a Major Action, rather than a Minor Action, to move or change position. See [Impede](../actions/impede.md).

## Block

A character may **Block** an appropriate physical attack with a shield or suitable armor. Equipment may add a Benefit to Body Defense, and some attacks can only be Blocked or Parried. See [Defenses](attributes/defenses.md#dodge-block-and-parry) and [Armor and Shields](equipment/armor-and-shields.md).

## Parry

A character may **Parry** an appropriate physical attack with a rigid weapon or suitable equipment. Some equipment adds a Benefit to Body Defense when used to Parry. See [Defenses](attributes/defenses.md#dodge-block-and-parry) and [Weapons](equipment/weapons.md).
