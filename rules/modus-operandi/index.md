# Core MOs

* [Cleric](cleric.md) - A divine champion and faith-based spellcaster.
* [Druid](druid.md) - A guardian drawing power from the natural world.
* [Mountebank](mountebank.md) - A charismatic trickster and social specialist.
* [Ranger](ranger.md) - A wilderness explorer, hunter, and guide.
* [Scholar](scholar.md) - An expert relying on knowledge and careful study.
* [Sorcerer](sorcerer.md) - An innate and personality-driven spellcaster.
* [Thief](thief.md) - A specialist in stealth, infiltration, traps, and locks.
* [Warrior](warrior.md) - A trained weapon and battlefield specialist.
* [Wizard](wizard.md) - A spellcaster mastering magic through study.
