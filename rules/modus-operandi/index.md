# Modus Operandi

* [Modus Operandi](overview.md) - How Modus Operandi define a character's training, methods, scores, and specializations.

# Core MOs

* [Cleric](cleric.md) - A divine champion who serves the gods and wields faith-based magic.
* [Druid](druid.md) - A guardian of the natural world who draws power from nature.
* [Mountebank](mountebank.md) - A charismatic trickster who relies on deception, performance, and social skill.
* [Ranger](ranger.md) - A skilled wilderness explorer, hunter, and guide.
* [Scholar](scholar.md) - A learned expert who overcomes challenges through knowledge and careful study.
* [Sorcerer](sorcerer.md) - An innate spellcaster who channels magic through force of personality.
* [Thief](thief.md) - A stealthy specialist in infiltration, traps, locks, and misdirection.
* [Warrior](warrior.md) - A trained combatant skilled with weapons, armor, and battlefield tactics.
* [Wizard](wizard.md) - A scholarly spellcaster who masters magic through study and preparation.
