# Scrounger

## Primary Benefit

If the Adventure Guide rules the environment supports it, once per game day the character can scrounge up one meal worth of fresh food and water for themselves and the other Player Characters. No Action Check is required.

## Secondary Benefit

If the Adventure Guide rules the environment supports it, once per game day the character can scrounge up one meal worth of fresh food and water for one person. No Action Check is required.
