# Extraordinary Potential

## Primary Benefit

When creating this character, increase their Body, Mind, or Spirit Primary Attribute maximums by 2.

## Secondary Benefit

When creating this character, increase their Body, Mind, or Spirit Primary Attribute maximums by 1.
