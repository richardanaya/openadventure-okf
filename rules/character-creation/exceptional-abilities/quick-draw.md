# Quick Draw

## Primary Benefit

Unless surprised, your character always acts first in the first Round of an Encounter. They also gain a +1 Effect to Reaction Checks to determine Turn order.

## Secondary Benefit

The character gains a +1 Effect to Reaction Checks to determine Turn order.
