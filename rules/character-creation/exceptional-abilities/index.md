# Exceptional Abilities

* [But I Get Up Again](but-i-get-up-again.md) - Restores one or all Body, Mind, and Spirit Point pools to maximum once per game session.
* [Extraordinary Potential](extraordinary-potential.md) - Raises the starting maximums for one group of Body, Mind, or Spirit Primary Attributes.
* [Follower](follower.md) - Grants a valet or torchbearer who performs mundane tasks, with the torchbearer willing to enter dangerous situations.
* [Hard to Kill](hard-to-kill.md) - Extends the time available to stabilize while Dying and can improve Death Checks.
* [Heightened Senses](heightened-senses.md) - Grants a Benefit to Reaction Checks using one specific sense or to all Reaction Checks.
* [Increased Vitality](increased-vitality.md) - Raises one Body, Mind, or Spirit Point pool and can improve Recovery Checks for that pool.
* [Lucky](lucky.md) - Allows the character to reroll a roll and, at primary strength, to force an opposing NPC to reroll.
* [Quick Draw](quick-draw.md) - Improves Reaction Checks for turn order and, at primary strength, makes the character act first unless surprised.
* [Natural Armor](natural-armor.md) - Adds Armor to Body, Mind, or Spirit, either always or only while no other armor is worn.
* [Reputation](reputation.md) - Grants a Benefit to Presence Checks with people who share the character's background or culture.
* [Scrounger](scrounger.md) - Provides food and water once per game day when the environment supports foraging.
* [Spellcaster](spellcaster.md) - Allows the character to cast spells, with doubled point loss on failed checks at secondary strength.
